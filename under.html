Under-Life of a Frog is the first game made in Java by WiErD0 Games, made during Ludum Dare 29 for the theme "Beneath the Surface". In Under, you are a frog that has to survive, while getting as many points as possible. With infinite, randomly generated terrain, you can explore the depths of the sea or the tallest mountains to find treasure, or stick to smaller islands and eat flies for food. Good luck and don't drown! : )


Download Here!

Controls:

Turn: A/D

Jump off an island: Hold space

Swim up: Tap space

Swim down: Hold S, Tap space
