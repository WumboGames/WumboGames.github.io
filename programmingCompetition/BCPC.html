Taking Part in the Brookfield Computer Programming Challenge? Here are the files you need!

Get PC^2 the software we will be using to run the BCPC 2017.

What do I need to do?

  1. Download the PC^2 zip from the above link.
  2. Unzip what you just downloaded.
  3. If you are in the practice competition, open bin/PC^2.ini and change the line that says "server=localhost:50002" to whatever we tell you to
  4. Double click bin/pc2team.bat to log in. We will give you your usernames and passwords.
